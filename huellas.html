Huellas Eternas

Uprisign Role: Developer FrontEnd /UX /Usability testing

This project was a challenge to find a way to connect interaction design with the conservation and dissemination of the art and culture of Tocancipá among young people between 18 and 25 years old. Looking for the way in which people could access this experience from their mobile devices eliminating barriers to download native applications.

Communicative Intent

Huellas eternas provides an experience of dissemination of the works preserved by the Casa de la Cultura de Tocancipá, using Augmented Reality technology. Through a web portal, users can explore each work and deepen their connection with any of them through interactive spaces specially designed to stimulate cultural interest in the youth of Tocancipá, this system not only promotes art and culture but also encourages learning in a more interactive way among young people, creating a deeper connection with cultural memory.

Tools implemented

The project incorporates cutting-edge tools to deliver a seamless and immersive experience. Needle Engine enables advanced interactivity in augmented reality rooms, enhancing user engagement. Webflow streamlines web development with an intuitive interface, ensuring efficient design and structuring. Meanwhile, Model Viewer provides a robust solution for storing and visualizing 3D models on the web, while also integrating augmented reality functionalities for a truly immersive experience.
