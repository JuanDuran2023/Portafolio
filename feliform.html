Feliform

Uprisign Role: Developer UI / Video Editor /Sound Fx

This project was a challenge since an RPG videogame had never been made before, besides looking for a connection between the projects already made, this one was born as an expansion of the cat stories where users would see reflected the animal abuse without leaving aside the gameplay and the science fiction experience.

Communicative Intent

Feliform, a game that combines a powerful narrative with a unique grunge aesthetic. This role-playing adventure game will take you to explore the deep emotions of three sibling cats struggling to overcome the trauma of abuse and confront a malevolent entity that feeds on their suffering.

Tools implemented

For the realization of the sound environment was done through Reaper and then this was implemented with Fmod for the connection with Unity through parameters. For the creation of the prototypes of the space was done through blender and the creation of the Teaser was implemented with Premiere.
