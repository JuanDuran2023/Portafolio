6420+|Seends of Memory

Uprisign Role: Developer FrontEnd /UX /Usability testing

6402+ | Seeds of Memory is a digital memorial that honors the victims of extrajudicial killings in Colombia, known as “false positives.” The project is named after the number of documented cases and uses the metaphor of a dandelion to represent each victim as a seed that stays alive in our collective memory.

Huellas Eternas

Uprisign Role: Developer FrontEnd /UX /Usability testing

Huellas eternas provides an experience of dissemination of the works preserved by the Casa de la Cultura de Tocancipá, using Augmented Reality technology. Through a web portal, users can explore each work and deepen their connection with any of them through interactive spaces specially designed to stimulate cultural interest in the youth of Tocancipá, this system not only promotes art and culture but also encourages learning in a more interactive way among young people, creating a deeper connection with cultural memory.
